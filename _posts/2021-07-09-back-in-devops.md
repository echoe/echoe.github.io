So. I quit my job, did the things I said I'd do. But then I also ... just kept interviewing? And in the middle of recovering from surgery, I ... got another job.
I'm really torn on if I like it or not. I enjoy the money, but a lot of the process is really ... arduous. And I've gotten ... tired of it.

It feels like I don't know how to take a break. Or, more realistically, like the job itself, generally, burns people out and makes taking a break seem impossible.
And the things I'm doing right now are ... there's a large synthesis of things that I've had to learn.
Having to learn a ton of different things that are undescribed generally doesn't combine well with imposter syndrome (which everybody in the space has, 100%), and it doubly doesn't combine well with being a minority in a tech space in every way possible (which is admittedly extremely rare for me! Wow! This sucks!).

... So. Money is good, I guess? I'll continue to do it until I literally can't, maybe. Or I can figure out how to manage my burnout better. We'll see.
